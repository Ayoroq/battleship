2. Fleet Setup

When all parts of a ship are hit, it is destroyed and revealed.

5. Victory

The first commander to destroy all enemy ships wins the battle.

Turn Limit: If both fleets survive after 50 turns (25 turns per commander), the commander with more ships remaining wins the battle.
